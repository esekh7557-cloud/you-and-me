About Us

Family-oriented dining with Indian comfort and Goan character

You and Me Multi Cuisine brings together comforting Indian classics, fresh seafood, and the easy warmth that makes dining out in Goa feel memorable.

Our Story

Created for shared tables, generous flavours, and welcoming service

At You and Me Multi Cuisine, we believe the best restaurants feel both special and familiar. That means a menu with enough variety for every table, flavours that stay true to the dish, and service that keeps the experience relaxed from arrival to the final bite.

Our multi-cuisine approach is rooted in balance: Indian favourites for comfort, seafood for the coastal connection, and an atmosphere that suits family dinners, celebrations, and easy holiday meals in Arpora.

  • Fresh ingredients chosen for flavour and consistency
  • Recipes inspired by familiar favourites and coastal dining
  • Warm Goan hospitality that keeps guests coming back
